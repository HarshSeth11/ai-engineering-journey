LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be applied for at least 2 weeks in advance. Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days. Sick leave is separate and allows up to 10 days per year with a medical certificate.

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. A stable internet connection and a dedicated workspace are required. Employees must be available during core hours of 10am to 4pm. Equipment provided by the company must be used for work purposes only.

TRAVEL POLICY
Business travel must be approved by the department head. Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours. Business class is allowed for flights over 6 hours. Hotel accommodation is reimbursed up to $150 per night. Meal expenses are reimbursed up to $50 per day with receipts.

MEDICAL INSURANCE POLICY
All full-time employees are covered under the company medical insurance plan. Coverage includes hospitalization, outpatient treatment, and dental care. Dependents including spouse and up to 2 children can be added to the plan. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of treatment with original receipts.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year in June and December. Employees are rated on a scale of 1 to 5 across key competencies. A rating of 3 or above is required to be eligible for annual increment. Employees rated below 3 for two consecutive reviews may be placed on a performance improvement plan.
