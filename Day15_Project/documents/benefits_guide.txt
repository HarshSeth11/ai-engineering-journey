HEALTH BENEFITS
All full-time employees receive comprehensive health coverage from day one. The company covers 80% of premium costs for the employee and 60% for dependents. Vision and dental are included in the base plan at no extra cost.

RETIREMENT PLAN
The company offers a 401k plan with up to 4% employer matching. Employees are eligible after 6 months of employment. Vesting period is 2 years for employer contributions.

WELLNESS BENEFITS
Employees receive a $500 annual wellness allowance for gym memberships, fitness equipment, or mental health services. The company also offers 5 paid mental health days per year in addition to regular sick leave.
