CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are strictly prohibited. Violations must be reported to HR within 24 hours. Repeated violations may result in termination.

DRESS CODE
Business casual attire is required Monday to Thursday. Casual attire is permitted on Fridays. Client-facing meetings require formal business attire regardless of day. Employees must wear their ID badges at all times on company premises.

CONFIDENTIALITY POLICY
All company information, client data, and business strategies are strictly confidential. Employees must sign an NDA before accessing sensitive systems. Sharing confidential information externally without approval is grounds for immediate termination.
